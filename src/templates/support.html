Serhiy Prytula Foundation

Serhiy Prytula is a ukrainian actor, comedian and television host who has been helping the Ukrainian Army as a private volunteer since Russia occupied Crimea and Donbas in 2014. In February 2022, after Russia launched a full-scale invasion, the Foundation joined the Army supply movement, covering the needs of both the Armed Forces fighters and members of Territorial Defense units. The Foundation buys protective gear, optic devices, drones, medical supplies, and transport vehicles for the military. Another direction they work on is humanitarian aid.

Come Back Alive Foundation

The foundation supports the Armed Forces of Ukraine through financing purely defence initiatives. Since 2014 we have provided around 1000 thermal imagers and over 250 UAVs. In addition to the material support, we increased technological capabilities of the Army through providing 1,500 tablets with Armor software aimed at stopping the artillery. Come Back Alive also organizes trainings to equip our military with crucial skills. In 2015 we launched trainings for medical workers, artillerists, snipers, minesweepers, and defence analysts. After coming back from the front line, our military members have access to sport rehabilitation and veteran business opportunities.
